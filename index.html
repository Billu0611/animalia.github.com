Welcome to Animalia.com

Animalia is a non-governmental organization.

Here we provide shelter and nutritious food to stray and homeless animals in India. While cattle, dogs and cats are the main categories of animals at our shelters, birds, white mice, squirrels, horses, donkeys, deer and even a monitor lizard have temporary refuge with us.
